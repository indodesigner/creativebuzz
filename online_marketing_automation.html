Our Strategies

With any marketing or any business operation, we maximize performance by leveraging the advantages of the platform. The online world never sleeps, your website is there to take orders 24 hours a day, and to make the most of that, your marketing needs to work the same way. Few of us have the resources to keep teams working around the clock though, but with automation, there is an answer that, lets every business enjoy the benefits of constant promotion without the expense

Google Ads

AdWords is Google’s ad program, delivering your brand and message to people actively looking at content related to your product or service. By carefully choosing keywords, ads can be precisely targeted, and because you only pay when someone clicks, offer efficient use of your marketing budget too.

Google Remarketing

Remarketing allows you to market to people who have already shown an interest in your brand before, whether that is your website or offer page. With carefully structured remarketing campaigns from CreativeBuzz, you can have another chance to convert prospects into buyers, boosting revenue.

PPC and CPC ads

While Google dominates the market, there are many options for PPC and CPC ad solutions. We will identify the best platforms for your unique situation and develop effective, engaging ad content that delivers the traffic you need.

Social Media ads

From Facebook to YouTube, most social media platforms have dedicated ad programs that allow you to precisely target demographics and interests, putting your message in front of exactly who you want. Our program will organize the entire process from design to implementation and optimization once the campaign is live.

Campaign Management

Whether your campaign involves just one marketing option or brings all of them together as an interlinked marketing approach, we will handle every aspect of the process for you. From designing ads to selecting keywords, monitoring performance, and testing changes, we can take care of every digital marketing need you have.
